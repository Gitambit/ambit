STAGE 1: CONCEPT DESIGN

Step 1 - Software Risk Classification

  • Activity Summary: Classify software based on regulatory requirements and assign risk classes (A, B, C).
  • Inputs: DHF-XX-IUD Intended Purpose & Product Description.
  • Outputs: DHF-XX-CLR Classification Report.

STAGE 2: DESIGN PLANNING

Step 1 - Define Device Safety Characteristics

  • Activity Summary: Analyze intended use to define device safety characteristics.
  • Inputs: DHF-XX-IUD Intended Purpose & Product Description.
  • Outputs: F-710-001 Initial Risk Analysis Part A table 1.

Step 2 - Evaluate Severity of Harm

  • Activity Summary: Evaluate severity of harm without risk control measures.
  • Inputs: DHF-XX-IUD Intended Purpose & Product Description, F-710-001 Initial Risk Analysis Part A table 1.
  • Outputs: F-710-001 Initial Risk Analysis Part A table 2, 3, and 4.

Step 3 - Populate Risk Analysis Part B

  • Activity Summary: Populate F-710-001 Initial Risk Analysis Part B with anticipated misuse scenarios.
  • Inputs: DHF-XX-SOA State of the Art document, DHF-XX-IUD Intended Purpose & Product Description Document, DHF-XX-BUI Business case document, DHF-XX-UND User Needs document.
  • Outputs: F-710-001 Initial Risk Analysis Part B.

Step 4 - Identify Hazard Categories

  • Activity Summary: Identify and justify hazard categories at each device life cycle stage.
  • Inputs: DHF-XX-SOA State of the Art document, DHF-XX-IUD Intended Purpose & Product Description Document, DHF-XX-BUI Business case document, DHF-XX-UND User Needs document.
  • Outputs: F-710-001 Initial Risk Analysis Part C.

Step 5 - Hazardous Situations, Harm Types, and Root Cause

  • Activity Summary: Perform comprehensive risk analysis for each hazard, including hazard sequences of events, hazardous situations, harm types (person, environment, property), and root causes.
  • Inputs: F-710-001 Initial Risk Analysis Part C, DHF-XX-IUD Intended Purpose & Product Description Document, DHF-XX-UND User Needs document, DHF-XX-BUI Business case document.
  • Outputs: F-710-002 FMEA table up to 'SW defect categories' field.

Step 6 - Determine Occurrence of Harm

  • Activity Summary: Determine initial occurrence of harm (O) for every single risk.
  • Inputs: F-710-001 Initial Risk Analysis Part D.
  • Outputs: F-710-002 FMEA table up to 'occurrence of harm (O)' field.

Step 7 - Evaluate Severity of Harm

  • Activity Summary: Determine the severity of harm (S) for every single risk and calculate its risk score (R).
  • Inputs: F-710-001 Initial Risk Analysis Part A Table 2, 3, and 4.
  • Outputs: Complete fields in the F-710-002 FMEA table for 'Severity of harm (S)' and 'risk score (R)' field.

Step 8 - Analyze Risk Control Measures

  • Activity Summary: Analyze each identified risk to determine suitable risk control measures.
  • Inputs: F-710-002 FMEA table up to 'Risk score (R)' field.
  • Outputs: F-710-005 Individual Risk review meeting for each risk.

Step 9 - Update SRS Design Documentation

  • Activity Summary: Update or create new SRS requirements as risk control measures.
  • Inputs: F-710-005 Individual Risk review meeting for each risk.
  • Outputs: DHF-XX-SRS Software Requirements Specification.

Step 10 - Review Risk Management Activities

  • Activity Summary: Review risk management activities at this life cycle stage.
  • Inputs: Step 1 to 9.
  • Outputs: DHF-XX-MRI Minute Meeting Review - Design Input lock.

STAGE 3: PRODUCT DESIGN

Step 1 - Identify Software Architecture Segregation

  • Activity Summary: Identify architectural segregations necessitated by risk control and update SRS documentation accordingly.
  • Inputs: F-710-002 FMEA table up to 'Risk score (R)' field, DHF-XX-SRS Software Requirements Specification.
  • Outputs: DHF-XX-SAD Software Architecture Document.

Step 2 - Risk Related to SOUP (Software of Unknown Provenance)

  • Activity Summary: Identify Software of Unknown Provenance (SOUP) elements, analyze their corresponding anomaly list, and update FMEA table and SRS documentation if necessary.
  • Inputs: DHF-XX-SAD Software Architecture Document, DHF-XX-SSS SOUP Software Specification, DHF-XX-SDP Software Development Plan, F-710-002 FMEA table.
  • Outputs: DHF-XX-SSS SOUP Software Specification.

Step 3 - Categorization of Adopted Risk Control Measures

  • Activity Summary: Describe within the FMEA table the risk control measures adopted and specify whether they are by design, protective measures, or information for safety. Finally, trace associated SRS requirements.
  • Inputs: DHF-XX-SRS Software Requirements Specification, DHF-XX-SAD Software Architecture Document, DHF-XX-SDD Software Detailed Design, DHF-XX-SSS SOUP Software Specification, DHF-XX-SDS Software Design Specification, DHF-XX-UEF Usability Engineering File.
  • Outputs: F-710-002 FMEA table up to 'Risk Control Measure adopted' field.

Step 4 - Iterative Risk Management during Product Design

  • Activity Summary: Iteratively update design documentation (SRS, SAD, SDD, SOUP Specification) based on risk control measures and new risks identified during Software Unit Verification, Integration, or Usability testing.
  • Inputs: DHF-XX-UVR Software Unit Verification Plan & Report, DHF-XX-SIP Software Integration Plan & Integration Testing, DHF-XX-UEF Usability Engineering File, F-710-002 FMEA table.
  • Outputs: DHF-XX-UVR Software Unit Verification Plan & Report, DHF-XX-SIP Software Integration Plan & Integration Testing, DHF-XX-UEF Usability Engineering File.

Step 5 - Risk Management File Update

  • Activity Summary: Review the risk management file and verify that risk assessment was addressed for Software Unit Verification, Integration, or Usability testing.
  • Inputs: DHF-XX-UVR Software Unit Verification Plan & Report, DHF-XX-SIP Software Integration Plan & Integration Testing, DHF-XX-UEF Usability Engineering File, F-710-002 FMEA table.
  • Outputs: DHF-XX-MRI Minute Meeting Review.

STAGE 4: DESIGN VERIFICATION

Step 1 - Evaluating Failed Tests and Managing Risks

  • Activity Summary: Failed results during the verification tests are reported in the anomaly report. Each issue is evaluated for its impact on SW safety and performance. Any new identified risk is treated following the procedure of risk management.
  • Inputs: F-730-027 DHF-XX-SVR Software System Verification Report, F-730-029 DHF-XX-CYB Cybersecurity Document.
  • Outputs: F-730-030 DHF-XX-SAR Software Anomalies Report, F-710-003 DHF-XX-RMA Risk Management Assessment.

Step 2 - Individual Risk Review for Risk Control Implementation

  • Activity Summary: Conduct individual review meetings for each identified risk to discuss risk control options. Analyze the following: Verification of implementation 7.2.
  • Inputs: F-730-027 DHF-XX-SVR Software System Verification Report, F-730-029 DHF-XX-CYB Cybersecurity Document, F-730-030 DHF-XX-SAR Software Anomalies Report.
  • Outputs: F-710-005 Individual Risk review meeting for each risk.

STAGE 5: DESIGN VALIDATION

Step 1 - Addressing Validation Issues and Risk Management

  • Activity Summary: Issues may be detected during the validation studies and are reported in the anomaly report. Each issue is evaluated for its impact on SW safety and performance. Any new identified risk is treated following the procedure of risk management.
  • Inputs: F-730-033 DHF-XX-SVA Software Validation Activities, F-730-039 DHF-XX-TPR Software Technical Performance Validation Report, F-730-040 DHF-XX-USR Software Usability Validation Report.
  • Outputs: F-730-030 DHF-XX-SAR Software Anomalies Report, F-710-003 DHF-XX-RMA Risk Management Assessment.

Step 2 - Individual Risk Review for Risk Control Effectiveness

  • Activity Summary: Conduct individual review meetings for each identified risk to discuss risk control options. Analyze the following: Verification of effectiveness 7.2.
  • Inputs: F-730-033 DHF-XX-SVA Software Validation Activities, F-730-039 DHF-XX-TPR Software Technical Performance Validation Report, F-730-040 DHF-XX-USR Software Usability Validation Report, F-730-030 DHF-XX-SAR Software Anomalies Report.
  • Outputs: F-710-005 Individual Risk review meeting for each risk, F-710-004 DHF-XX-RMR Software Risk Management Report.

STAGE 6: DESIGN TRANSFER & SW DEPLOYMENT

Step 1 - Verifying Release Note and Safety Information

  • Activity Summary: Verify that residual risks and warnings are stipulated in the Release note and instruction for use.
  • Inputs: F-710-003 DHF-XX-RMA Risk Management Assessment, F-730-030 DHF-XX-SAR Software Anomalies Report.
  • Outputs: F-730-045 DHF-XX-SRN Software Release Note, F-730-034 DHF-XX-IFU Instruction For Use.

Step 2 - Validating Processes and Managing Risks

  • Activity Summary: Identify any issue and new risk associated with software deployment process, master copy, virtual transport, or any required activity for production. Any new identified risk is treated following the procedure of risk management.
  • Inputs: F-730-027 DHF-XX-SVR Software System Verification Report, F-730-028 DHF-XX-STM Software Traceability Matrix, F-730-029 DHF-XX-CYB Cybersecurity Document, F-710-003 DHF-XX-RMA Risk Management Assessment.
  • Outputs: F-730-030 DHF-XX-SAR Software Anomalies Report, F-710-003 DHF-XX-RMA Risk Management Assessment.

Step 3 - Individual Final Risk Review

  • Activity Summary: Conduct individual review meetings for each identified risk to discuss risk control options. Analyze the following: Residual risk acceptability 7.3, Benefit-risk ratio 7.4, Risk arising from risk control measures 7.5, Severity of harm (S) after risk control, Occurrence of harm (O) after risk control, Risk score (R) after risk control.
  • Inputs: F-710-003 DHF-XX-RMA Risk Management Assessment.
  • Outputs: F-710-005 Individual Risk review meeting for each risk, F-710-004 DHF-XX-RMR Software Risk Management Report.

Step 4 - Verifying Risk Management Report Correctness

  • Activity Summary: Verify correctness and completeness of the SW risk management report and risk management table before software release.
  • Inputs: F-710-004 DHF-XX-RMR Software Risk Management Report, F-710-003 DHF-XX-RMA Risk Management Assessment.
  • Outputs: F-730-048 DHF-XX-MRR Review of Design Output & SW Release.

STAGE 6: PRODUCT LAUNCH

Step 1 - Addressing Notified Body Inquiries

  • Activity Summary: Answer notified bodies or health agencies' questions or non-conformity concerning product risk management.
  • Inputs: F-710-004 DHF-XX-RMR Software Risk Management Report, F-710-003 DHF-XX-RMA Risk Management Assessment.
  • Outputs: Product Technical File.

STAGE 7: POST MARKET

Step 1 - Managing Post-Market Risks and Feedback

  • Activity Summary: Identify any new risks from the collection of post-market information, including customer feedback. Any new identified risk is treated following the procedure of risk management.
  • Inputs: F-710-004 DHF-XX-RMR Software Risk Management Report, F-710-003 DHF-XX-RMA Risk Management Assessment.
  • Outputs: F-710-004 DHF-XX-RMR Software Risk Management Report, Technical File.

Step 2 - Verifying Release Note and Safety Information

  • Activity Summary: Verify that residual risks and warnings are stipulated in the Release note and instruction for use before any new software release.
  • Inputs: F-710-004 DHF-XX-RMR Software Risk Management Report, F-710-003 DHF-XX-RMA Risk Management Assessment.
  • Outputs: F-730-045 DHF-XX-SRN Software Release Note, F-730-034 DHF-XX-IFU Instruction For Use.
